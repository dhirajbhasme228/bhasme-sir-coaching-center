Meet Bhasme Sir

A distinguished educator with 35 years of service, Bhasme Sir is the retired Headmaster of Nagar Parishad High School & Jr. College, Katol, and an awarded Best Maths Teacher. Under his guidance, generations of students have mastered mathematics and gone on to become doctors, engineers, lawyers, administrative officers and respected professionals.

His approach clears fundamental concepts first, then builds speed and accuracy through structured practice. Every student gets personal attention, expert guidance, regular feedback, and a clear roadmap to score well in school and SSC board exams.
